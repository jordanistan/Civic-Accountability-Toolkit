# Donald J. Trump

## Overview
As President, Donald Trump has issued a series of executive orders that have been met with both administrative implementation and significant legal challenges in federal courts.

## Documented Legal / Constitutional / Ethics Concerns

### Birthright Citizenship Executive Order (EO 14160)
- **Date:** January 20, 2025
- **Type:** Executive Action / Constitutional Dispute
- **Summary:** Signed an order attempting to end the granting of birthright citizenship to children of non-citizens.
- **Possible Issue:** 14th Amendment (Citizenship Clause) compliance.
- **Status:** Nationwide injunctions in place; pending SCOTUS review (2026).
- **Link:** [[incidents/2026-birthright-citizenship-litigation|2026-birthright-citizenship-litigation]]

### Operation Epic Fury (Iran Strikes)
- **Date:** February 28, 2026
- **Type:** Military Action / Oversight Dispute
- **Summary:** Authorized strikes on Iranian targets without a specific new Congressional AUMF.
- **Possible Issue:** War Powers Resolution of 1973.
- **Status:** Subject to ongoing Congressional inquiry.
- **Link:** [[incidents/2026-iran-war-powers-dispute|2026-iran-war-powers-dispute]]

## Suggested Oversight Ask
Citizens may request their **Representative** to seek a formal legal opinion from the **Office of Legal Counsel (OLC)** regarding the statutory authority for "Operation Epic Fury" and whether it complies with the 60-day notification requirements of the War Powers Act.
