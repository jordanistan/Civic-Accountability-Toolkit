# FAQ

## Is this a legal filing?
No. It is a public-record and oversight toolkit.

## Does this repo declare anyone guilty?
No. It separates court findings, documented allegations, and open questions.

## Can I use this to contact Congress?
Yes, but keep messages factual, respectful, and tied to sources.

## Can I open this in Obsidian?
Yes. The repo is designed to work as a linked Markdown vault.
