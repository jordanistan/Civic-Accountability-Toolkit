# Sourcing Standard

## Use this order of preference
1. Official records and court orders
2. Major outlet reporting with document support or named sourcing
3. Expert letters, watchdogs, NGOs
4. Opinion and commentary only as context

## Red lines
- Do not state a crime occurred unless the source supports that level of certainty.
- Do not equate allegations with findings.
- Do not use social media claims as primary proof.

## Every incident should answer
- What happened?
- Who was involved?
- What source supports this?
- What remains unknown?
