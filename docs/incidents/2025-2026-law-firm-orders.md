# 2025–2026 law firm orders litigation

## One-line summary
Court challenges to executive actions targeting law firms raised First and Fifth Amendment issues.

Multiple federal judges reportedly struck down or blocked administration actions targeting law firms, with rulings discussing free speech and due-process concerns.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
The final appellate posture and any individual criminal liability remain unresolved.
