# 14th Amendment: Citizenship Clause

## Legal Summary
The first sentence of the 14th Amendment establishes the principle of *jus soli* (right of the soil). The Supreme Court has historically upheld this in cases like *United States v. Wong Kim Ark* (1898).

## Current Relevance
Under the 2025 administration, EO 14160 seeks to reinterpret "subject to the jurisdiction thereof" to exclude children of undocumented residents. This is currently under litigation.
