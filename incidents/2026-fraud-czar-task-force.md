# 2026 Fraud Czar Task Force

## One-line summary
March 2026 appointment of Vice President JD Vance to lead a federal task force targeting alleged administrative fraud in "blue states."

## What is documented
Vice President JD Vance was appointed in March 2026 to lead the "Task Force to Eliminate Fraud." The task force is directed to audit state-level administrative records, specifically focusing on jurisdictions that have identified as "sanctuary" or non-compliant with certain federal directives.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
Legal experts and state officials contend this task force violates the **10th Amendment** (State Sovereignty) and the **Privacy Act**, as it seeks to compel the sharing of state-held sensitive data without traditional judicial oversight.

## Why it matters
This represents a potential expansion of federal oversight into state-level administration and benefit distribution.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request the charter and jurisdictional legal assessment for the Task Force to Eliminate Fraud and a list of all state data requests issued by the Vice President's office.
