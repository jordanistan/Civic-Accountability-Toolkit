# 2025 Signalgate Classified Sharing

## One-line summary
Allegations that high-ranking officials shared classified military data via encrypted Signal chats with unauthorized parties.

## What is documented
House Oversight Committee testimony in April 2026 identified an encrypted group chat containing JD Vance, Pete Hegseth, and others. Digital forensic snapshots provided by whistleblowers show the sharing of coordinates for Yemen airstrikes prior to official declassification.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
The extent of the distribution and whether the intent was for "leaking" or internal administrative convenience (which still violates handling protocols).

## Why it matters
Violation of 18 U.S.C. § 793 (Espionage Act) protocols regarding national defense information.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request a formal damage assessment from the DNI and a referral to the DOJ Public Integrity Section for unauthorized disclosure of classified information.
