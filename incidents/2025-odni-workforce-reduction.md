# 2025 ODNI Workforce Reduction

## One-line summary
25% reduction of the Office of the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI) workforce in 2025 as part of administration-wide downsizing.

## What is documented
Director Tulsi Gabbard, in collaboration with the Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE), implemented a 25% staff reduction at the ODNI during 2025.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
Intelligence professionals have raised concerns regarding the impact on national security readiness and whether the layoffs targeted specific analytic units for political reasons.

## Why it matters
Massive reductions in the intelligence community's administrative and analytic staff can impact the quality and timeliness of national threat assessments.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request a breakdown of the specific roles and divisions impacted by the 2025 ODNI layoffs and a risk assessment of the resulting loss in human intelligence capabilities.
