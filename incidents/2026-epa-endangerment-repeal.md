# 2026 EPA Endangerment Repeal

## One-line summary
February 2026 repeal of the 2009 EPA Endangerment Finding, removing the legal basis for federal greenhouse gas regulation.

## What is documented
EPA Administrator Lee Zeldin signed the final rule in February 2026. This action eliminates the requirement for the EPA to regulate carbon emissions under the Clean Air Act by asserting that greenhouse gases do not "endanger" public health.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
A coalition of 25 states alleges the EPA "fabricated" or cherry-picked scientific data to reach a pre-determined political outcome, violating the Administrative Procedure Act.

## Why it matters
This is a foundational shift in environmental law that affects federal authority over vehicle emissions and industrial pollutants.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request an Inspector General audit of the peer-review process for the scientific studies cited in the February 2026 repeal.
