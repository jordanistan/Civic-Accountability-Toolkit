# 2026 Intelligence Retractions Order

## One-line summary
February 2026 order by CIA Director John Ratcliffe to retract and revise previous intelligence assessments deemed "politically biased."

## What is documented
In February 2026, Director Ratcliffe issued a directive to the Directorate of Intelligence to retract several high-profile assessments, including those related to domestic extremism and foreign interference, asserting they failed to meet tradecraft standards.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
Whistleblowers and former analysts allege the retractions were politically motivated to remove assessments that were critical of the administration's allies or policy positions.

## Why it matters
The integrity of intelligence analysis is mandated by the **Intelligence Reform and Terrorism Prevention Act (IRTPA)**. Politicization of intelligence can lead to skewed policy-making.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request all internal CIA evaluations that supposedly identified the "analytic tradecraft failures" leading to the February 2026 retractions.
