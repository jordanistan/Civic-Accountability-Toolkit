# 2025 AFFH Rule Repeal

## One-line summary
Termination of the "Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing" (AFFH) rule by HUD in 2025.

## What is documented
HUD Secretary Scott Turner signed the directive terminating the AFFH rule in 2025, which required local governments to address patterns of housing discrimination and segregation.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
Litigants argue the repeal violates the **Fair Housing Act of 1968**, which mandates that the executive branch proactively work to eliminate housing discrimination.

## Why it matters
The repeal removes a primary mechanism for federal enforcement of fair housing standards at the municipal level.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request the Office of Legal Counsel (OLC) opinion regarding HUD's statutory obligation to "affirmatively further" fair housing following the rule's repeal.
