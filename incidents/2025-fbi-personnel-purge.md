# 2025 FBI Personnel Purge

## One-line summary
Mass termination and forced retirement of FBI agents and officials perceived as "insufficiently loyal" following Kash Patel's appointment as Director.

## What is documented
Since early 2025, Director Kash Patel has overseen the removal of dozens of career FBI agents, including many who worked on investigations involving Donald Trump. The administration describes this as "clearing the deep state."

## What remains alleged or unresolved
Fired agents allege the terminations were retaliatory and violated civil service protections. A class-action lawsuit (*Agents v. FBI*) was filed in March 2026.

## Why it matters
The independence of the FBI and the protection of career civil servants from political retribution are central to public integrity.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request all personnel records and internal emails regarding the "loyalty-based" termination criteria used by the Office of the Director since January 2025.
