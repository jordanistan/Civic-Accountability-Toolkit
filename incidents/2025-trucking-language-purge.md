# 2025 Trucking Language Purge

## One-line summary
2025 removal of 11,000 commercial drivers from the workforce based on new English proficiency enforcement standards.

## What is documented
Under Secretary Sean Duffy, the Department of Transportation removed over 11,000 drivers and shut down 7,500 CDL training schools in 2025, citing a strict interpretation of English language requirements.

## What remains alleged or unresolved
Labor groups contend the purge disproportionately targeted immigrant communities and violated due process by revoking existing valid licenses without individual hearings.

## Why it matters
This impacts the national supply chain and raises significant civil rights and due process concerns regarding occupational licensing.

## Suggested oversight ask
Request the legal basis for the retroactive revocation of CDL licenses and an impact assessment on the national trucking workforce.
