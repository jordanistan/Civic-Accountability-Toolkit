# Wikipedia Summary: Jamieson Greer

## Summary
Jamieson Lee Greer is an American trade attorney and former Air Force officer who has served as the United States trade representative since February 2025. Greer has additionally served as the acting special counsel of the United States since April 2025. He served as the acting director of the United States Office of Government Ethics from April to August 2025.
