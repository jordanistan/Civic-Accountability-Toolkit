# Wikipedia Summary: Lori Chavez-DeRemer

## Summary
Lori Michelle Chavez-DeRemer is an American politician and businesswoman who has served as the United States secretary of labor since 2025. A member of the Republican Party, Chavez-DeRemer served as the U.S. representative for Oregon's fifth congressional district from 2023 to 2025 and as the mayor of Happy Valley, Oregon from 2011 to 2019.
